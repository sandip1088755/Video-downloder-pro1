Privacy Policy — Video Downloader Pro

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Video Downloader Pro ("the App") is developed by Sandip Digital. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

1. No Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us or to any third party. We do not have servers that receive your data. The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details.

2. What Stays On Your Device

All app data — download history, favorites, settings, and downloaded media files — is stored locally on your device only. This data is never uploaded anywhere and is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App.

3. Network Access

The App connects to the internet only to:

  • Download the media file from the direct URL you provide, and
  • Check for app updates (if enabled in Settings).

We do not operate any analytics, advertising, or tracking service inside the App.

4. Permissions Used

  • Internet access — required to download files you request.
  • Notifications — to show download progress and completion.
  • Storage (older Android versions only) — to save downloaded files.

No permission is used for any purpose beyond what's described above.

5. Your Content

You are solely responsible for ensuring you have the right to download and use any media you access through the App. The App is intended only for downloading content you own, have created, or are otherwise authorized to download (for example, direct links to your own files or content licensed for download).

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
